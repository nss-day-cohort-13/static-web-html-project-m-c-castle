virtuFEELZ Culture

At virtuFEELZ it’s not uncommon for our developers to be unsure if they’re in virtual reality, or non-virtual reality (as we like to call it) at any given point. This state of constant confusion bodes well for our developers and their creativity. To further blur our constructs of reality, we have removed all clocks from our commune. Employees base their day off their own circadian rhythm.
